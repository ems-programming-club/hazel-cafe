Frequently Asked Questions

Looking for a quick answer? Here are the questions we get most often.

Are you open Mondays?

No — we're closed Mondays.

What are your hours?

Tue–Fri 7am–5pm. Sat–Sun 8am–4pm.

Where are you located?

159 St & Fraser Hwy, Fleetwood, Surrey.

Is there parking?

Yes — free parking behind the building.

Do you deliver?

No. Pickup only.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes. Flourless chocolate cake, GF banana bread, and macarons. Please note that we use a shared kitchen and are not certified gluten-free.

Do you have vegan options?

Yes. Oat milk is available and we usually have some vegan bakes available daily.

How do I order a custom cake?

We need at least 72 hours notice and a $25 deposit.

What cake flavours do you offer?

Vanilla, chocolate, hazelnut, and mango.

Can I order a cake for tomorrow?

Usually not. We require 72 hours notice.
