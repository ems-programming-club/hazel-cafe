Frequently Asked Questions

Looking for a quick answer? Here are the questions we hear most often.

Are you open Mondays?

No — we're closed Mondays. Open Tue–Fri 7am–5pm and Sat–Sun 8am–4pm.

What are your hours today?

Tue–Fri 7am–5pm. Weekends 8am–4pm. Closed Mondays.

Where are you located?

159 St & Fraser Hwy in Fleetwood, Surrey.

Is there parking?

Yes — free parking is available behind the building.

Do you deliver?

No. Pickup only at our Fleetwood location.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes. We offer flourless chocolate cake, GF banana bread, and macarons. Everything is made in a shared kitchen, so we are not certified gluten-free.

Are you nut-free?

No. Nut traces are possible in all our bakes.

Do you have vegan options?

Yes. Oat milk is available for all drinks and we usually have some vegan baking options daily.

What are your bestsellers?

Our cinnamon bun, butter croissant, and hazelnut latte.

How much is a coffee?

Drip coffee is $3. Lattes are $4.75. Our Hazelnut Latte is $5.25.

How much is a birthday cake?

6-inch cakes start at $32, 8-inch cakes start at $42, and 10-inch cakes start at $58.

How do I order a custom cake?

Give us 72 hours notice, leave a $25 deposit, and choose your flavour: vanilla, chocolate, hazelnut, or mango.

Can I put a message on the cake?

Yes. Messages can be up to 6 words long.

How much notice do you need for cakes?

At least 72 hours notice is required.

Do you do cupcakes?

Yes. $4 each or $42 per dozen.

Do you sell lunch?

Yes. Sandwiches ($9), soup of the day ($7), quiche slices ($6.50), and bagels with cream cheese ($5).

Can I pay by card?

Yes. We accept cash, debit, and credit cards.

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Yes — free customer Wi-Fi is available.

Is there seating?

Yes. About 18 seats inside and 2 small tables outside.

Can I order a cake for tomorrow?

Usually not. We require 72 hours notice for custom cake orders.
